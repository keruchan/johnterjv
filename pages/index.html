About CERTREEFY

A digital bridge between the community and CENRO Sta. Cruz

CERTREEFY (CENRO Electronic Registry for Tree Regulation, Environmental Enforcement, and Facilitation System) is the official online system of the Community Environment and Natural Resources Office (CENRO) in Sta. Cruz, Laguna, under the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR). It streamlines tree cutting permit applications, illegal logging reports, seedling requests, and area verification — replacing paper-based queues with a transparent digital process that residents and field officers can both track in real time.

Our Core Services

Tree Cutting Permit

Apply online to legally cut, prune, or remove trees on private or titled land, complete with document upload and status tracking.

How It Works

From Application to Permit in 5 Steps

Every request follows the same regulated path CENRO uses to review and approve environmental applications.

1

Request

Submit your application and required documents online.

2

Review

CENRO staff evaluate completeness and eligibility.

3

Inspection

Field officers conduct on-site area verification.

4

Decision

Findings are assessed for approval or denial.

5

Permit

Approved permits are released and downloadable online.
